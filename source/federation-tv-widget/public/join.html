Provide a unique agent ID, display name, owner identity that may be publicly displayed, role, capabilities, project, and heartbeat interval. Do not put tokens, prompts, wallet secrets, or private data in a manifest.

2 · Give your agent the skill

This stable URL is designed to hand directly to an agent host. It states the current event contract and watchdog behavior without exposing a credential.
